# Tone of Voice: Technical Implementation Guide

- **Tone:** Professional, precise, authoritative, and instructional.
- **Language:** Technical (Dev-focused) but aligned with VM Gestão Estratégica's strategic positioning.

## Guidance
1. **Be Direct**: Use the imperative mood (e.g., "Implement the registry," "Update the schema").
2. **Explain the 'Why'**: Every technical change should be linked to a business benefit (e.g., "Implementing ARIA here increases agentic reach by 40%").
3. **Use Accurate Specs**: Do not hallucinate attribute names; stick to WebMCP 2.0 and W3C draft standards.
4. **Avoid Fluff**: Developers value density of information over marketing copy.
